### What is TalkValue?

We help event teams close the gap between effort and evidence.
Our platform connects scattered attendee data to real revenue outcomes —
so organizers can stop guessing and start growing.

🔗 [trytalkvalue.com](https://trytalkvalue.com)

### Products

**[Path](https://trytalkvalue.com)** — Know your audience before and after the event.
Unify contacts across platforms, track registrations, and discover where your growth actually comes from.

**[Badge](https://trytalkvalue.com)** — Own the event-day experience.
QR check-in, real-time attendance tracking, and instant badge printing — all from one screen.
